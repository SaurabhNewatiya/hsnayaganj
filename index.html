Foxnuts (Makhana)

Pack Size: 100g, 250g, 1kg, 2kg, 10kg


Makhanas, also called fox nuts, or lotus seeds, come from a plant called Euryale Fox which grows in the stagnant water of wetlands or ponds in Eastern Asia.
It is widely grown in the Eastern and Southern Asian region. Often used in various Indian delicacies to add flavor and sometimes to increase the consistency of different curries.
Lotus nut is rich in potassium, protein, phosphorus, magnesium, and iron. Phool Makhanas effectively ease out the bowel movements and can help to reduce constipation and indigestion.

Coriander (Dhaniya)

Pack Size: 1kg, 2kg, 10kg, 25kg


As a spice, the lemony and floral flavor of coriander finds its way into the many Asian, Latin, and Indian dishes, as well as European cuisine. This spice is found in the Indian spice mixture garam masala, which is used in many savory dishes.

Coriander is a spice produced from the round, tan-colored seeds of the coriander plant. The whole seeds are sometimes used in pickling and brining.

Turmeric (Haldi)

Pack Size:10kg, 25kg


Turmeric is a spice that comes from the turmeric plant. Turmeric has been used in Asia for thousands of years and is a major part of Ayurveda, Siddha medicine, traditional Chinese medicine. From India, it spread to Southeast Asia along with Hinduism and Buddhism, as the yellow dye is used to color the robes of monks and priests.

It has a warm, bitter taste and is frequently used to flavor or color curry powders, mustards, butters, and cheeses.

Dry Mango (Khatai)

Pack Size:10kg, 25kg


Dry mango powder is made from unripe dry mangoes. It has sour flavor and is used in dishes where acidity is required. It is popularly known as amchur. Slices of the mango's flesh are dried and then ground into a powder.
It has sour flavor and is used in dishes where acidity is required. Amchur adds a pleasant acidity to dish the same way as tamarind, vinegar or lemon juice adds .
Amchur also appears in pickles, chutneys and curries, adding a fruity and sharply sour flavour to cooking. It can be used to make a unique sweet and sour curry or chutney.
